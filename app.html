About

Presenting an innovative energy-saving application tailored for classroom environments. This application employs precise student headcount technology to intelligently control the operation of fans. By accurately detecting the number of students in the classroom, the application ensures optimal energy consumption by activating fans when needed and turning them off when the classroom is empty. This smart approach not only enhances comfort but also minimizes unnecessary energy usage, showcasing a step towards a greener and more efficient future for educational spaces.
